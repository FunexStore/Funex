Privacy Policy

How we handle your data with transparency and care.

This policy explains how Funex Studio collects, stores and uses any information you give when using this website or when engaging our services.

Information is used to provide services, communicate about your project, invoices and support. We will not sell or share personal data with third parties except where required by law or where third parties provide service under contract (e.g., payment processors).

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data. For sensitive file transfers, we provide secure upload links. Keep local backups of master files — we recommend using your own cloud storage as well.
